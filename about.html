About Geddes Lake

Community

Geddes Lake is a diverse community of decades-long residents, first-time homeowners, young families, and students.

The HOA is managed on-site by dedicated, full-time staff who understand the community – not an external management company. We have an elected board and several committees, all run by residents who volunteer to keep things running fairly and efficiently.

We host a series of regular events including board game nights, yard sale, fall festival, trick-or-treating, ice cream socials, garden tours, volunteer dinners, and more.

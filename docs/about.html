About Geddes Lake

Community

Geddes Lake is a diverse community with a mix of decades-long residents and first-time homeowners.

On-site, full-time staff provide responsive, local management – something many HOAs outsource. We have an elected board and several committees, all run by residents who volunteer to keep things running fairly and efficiently.

Regular events – from huge yard sales to regular board game nights – help maintain an active, connected atmosphere.

Amenities

Three ponds and extensive native gardens create a wonderfully green, quiet setting for somewhere so close to the city. Our 65 total acres include:

  • Rentable clubhouse for meetings and events
  • Playground
  • Pool with locker rooms and showers
  • Multipurpose tennis courts
  • Walkable surroundings – including to Gallup Park, Huron High School, and Ann Arbor bus stops
  • Located along the Huron Parkway bike path

Floorplans

Our 360 total units include four base floorplans, visible on the photos page:

  • Courtyard (2 bed, 1.5 bath, 1230 sq ft)

  • Terrace (3 bed, 2.5 bath, 1515 sq ft)
